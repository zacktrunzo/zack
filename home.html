About Me

I’m extremely passionate about telling stories with video! I first began creating videos at the young age of 9. Later creating several short films, feature films and then joining the Village Media / Accretive team focusing on corporate videos, event capture, live streaming, commercials and more. Whether you’re a business owner, promoting a product or even have a personal brand I look forward to hearing about how I can help tell YOUR story.

Projects

One of my recent works includes "Creator Economy," a feature-length documentary that explores the lives of three Algoma-based content creators with large social media followings.

I have also collaborated on various commercial promotions, including "46 Parallel."
